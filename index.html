About this project

Behind
GameBeeper.

GameBeeper is an open-source video game news aggregator. It collects RSS feeds from 34 trusted gaming sources — official platform publishers, independent outlets, and industry reporters — into one fast, clean, distraction-free feed. No ads, no paywalls, no tracking without consent.

Privacy Policy Terms of Use

Your Free Video Game News Hub

GameBeeper is the go-to destination for gamers who want to stay up to date with the latest video game news, game reviews, new game releases, and gaming industry developments — all in one place. Instead of visiting dozens of websites separately, GameBeeper pulls the best gaming stories from 34 trusted sources into a single, clean, distraction-free feed.

PlayStation News & Reviews

Get the latest PS5 and PlayStation news directly from the PlayStation Blog alongside expert coverage from Push Square. From new PlayStation exclusives and firmware updates to PS5 game reviews and PlayStation Store deals — all curated in real-time.

Xbox News & Game Pass Updates

Stay on top of everything Xbox with official updates from Xbox Wire and in-depth coverage from Pure Xbox. Never miss an Xbox Game Pass addition, Xbox Series X/S release, or major Xbox first-party game announcement.

Nintendo Switch & Switch 2 News

Follow every Nintendo Switch and Nintendo Switch 2 announcement with stories from Nintendo Life and Nintendo Everything — covering Nintendo Direct events, new Switch games, eShop deals, and more.

PC Gaming News

Stay current with the PC gaming world through PC Gamer, Rock Paper Shotgun, and PCGamesN — covering Steam new releases, PC game reviews, graphics card benchmarks, and gaming hardware news.

Game Reviews from Top Critics

Read the latest game reviews from IGN, GameSpot, Rock Paper Shotgun, Nintendo Life, Push Square, and Pure Xbox — covering new games across all platforms as soon as reviews go live.

Esports & Competitive Gaming

Follow the competitive gaming scene with the latest esports news, tournament results, and player moves from Dot Esports — covering League of Legends, Valorant, Counter-Strike, and the wider esports world.

Indie Games & Developer Logs

Discover the best upcoming and newly released indie games through Siliconera, Indie Games Plus, and live developer updates from itch.io Devlogs — the freshest indie game news direct from the creators.

Gaming Industry & Hardware News

Follow the business side of gaming and the latest hardware technology with GamesIndustry.biz, Digital Foundry, and Tom's Hardware — covering GPU benchmarks, game sales data, studio acquisitions, and console technology deep-dives.
